Reporting, Reviews & Work Visibility
Why Reporting Is Critical

Good VAs do not wait to be asked:

“What have you done?”

They provide visibility proactively.

Types of Reports VAs Use

- Daily task updates

- Weekly summaries

- Pending task lists

- Blocker reports


Reports should be:

i. Clear

ii. Concise

iii. Honest


Simple Reporting Structure

A good report answers:

i. What was completed?

ii. What is in progress?

iii. What is blocked or pending?
