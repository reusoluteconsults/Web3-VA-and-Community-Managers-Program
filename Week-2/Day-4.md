Calendar Management & Scheduling
Why Calendar Skills Matter


Founders rely on VAs to:

- Manage schedules

- Avoid conflicts

- Respect time zones

- Set reminders correctly

Poor scheduling creates stress and confusion.


Calendar Best Practices

i. Always confirm time zones

ii. Avoid overlapping meetings

iii. Use clear event titles

iv. Add notes when necessary

v. Set reminders appropriately

 
Common Scheduling Mistakes

- Assuming time zones

- Scheduling without confirmation

- Forgetting buffer time

- Poor naming of events
