Async Collaboration & Time Zone Management
Global Teams, Global Challenges


Web3 teams are global by default.

This means:

- Different time zones

- Different working hours

- Delayed responses


Async Communication Principles

i. Be clear and complete

ii. Avoid vague messages

iii. Document decisions

iv. Respect response time expectations

Good async communication prevents chaos.


Time Zone Awareness

i. Always confirm time zones

ii. Use UTC when possible

iii. Avoid assumptions

iv. Schedule thoughtfully
