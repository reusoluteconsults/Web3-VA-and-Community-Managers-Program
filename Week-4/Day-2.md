- Telegram: Real-time community communication

- X: Public voice of the project


Professional Conduct in Public Spaces

Always remember:

- You represent the project

- Screenshots last forever

- Tone shapes perception

- Every message matters.


Best Practices

i. Think before posting

ii. Use clear, respectful language

iii. Confirm information before sharing

iv. Avoid personal opinions in official spaces
