Professional Responsibility & Crisis Awareness
Public Mistakes Have Real Impact

In Web3:

- Mistakes spread quickly

- Silence can be damaging

- Poor responses escalate problems

You must act with care.


Handling Issues & Escalation

When unsure about something:

i. Do not guess

ii. Escalate to the right person

iii. Communicate clearly

iv. Stay calm and factual


Your Role in Crisis Situations

As a VA, you may:

i. Flag issues early

ii. Collect information

iii. Communicate updates

iv. Support community managers

You are not expected to solve everything - but you must respond responsibly.
