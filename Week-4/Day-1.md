How Work Actually Happens in Web3
Understanding Web3 Operations


Web3 teams do not operate like traditional companies.

Work happens across:

- Discord servers

- Telegram groups

- X (formerly Twitter)

- Shared documents

- Task boards

Much of this work is public-facing.


Why This Matters for Virtual Assistants

As a Web3 VA:

i. Your actions may be visible to thousands of people

ii. Mistakes can affect reputation instantly

iii. Speed and clarity matter

iv. There is less room for confusion.


Common Web3 Workflows includes;

i. Announcements posted publicly

ii. Community questions answered in real time

iii. Feedback gathered openly

iv. Decisions discussed in public channels
