Web3 Startups vs Web2 Companies
How Web3 Teams Are Different

Traditional (Web2) companies:

- Hierarchical

- Private operations

- Centralized decision-making

Web3 teams:

- Flat or semi-flat structures (everyone is almost equal)

- Public communication

- Community involvement (they carry everyone along)

- Transparency expectations


Why This Affects Your Role

In Web3:

i. Your messages may be public

ii. Community reactions matter

iii. Speed and tone are critical

iv. Mistakes are visible

This requires higher communication discipline.
