Web3 Culture, Language & Etiquette
Why Culture Matters in Web3

Web3 communities:

- Are highly vocal

- Value transparency

- React quickly to tone

- Distrust corporate language

Understanding culture prevents conflict.


Common Web3 Communication Norms

- Informal but respectful tone

- Direct communication

- Fast responses during active hours

- Public accountability

- Community-first mindset


Common Mistakes to Avoid

i. Over-corporate language

ii. Ignoring community concerns

iii. Talking down to users

iv. Over-promising

v. Silence during issues
